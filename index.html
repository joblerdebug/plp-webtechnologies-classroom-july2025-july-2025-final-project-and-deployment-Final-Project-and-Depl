Turn Waste Into Reliable, Clean Cooking Gas

Prefabricated biogas digesters convert the sanitary waste on-site into high‑pressure, clean gas piped to your kitchen.

1

Feed

We use the sanitary waste you already produce. Kitchen scraps and manure is optional.

2

Digest

Sealed tank automatically converts (digests) the waste to pure gas safely on-site. The gas is then stored for use.

3

Use

Purified gas piped to stoves — immediate LPG-like performance.
