Our Vision for Africa

We're building a future where energy independence empowers education across the continent

Educational Transformation

We see a future where no school's budget is drained by energy costs, where every shilling saved on firewood is redirected to books, teachers, and facilities.

Health & Safety

We envision school kitchens where staff work in dignity and safety, free from smoke inhalation and physical danger.

Environmental Sustainability

We picture schools as green leaders in their communities, demonstrating sustainable waste-to-energy solutions.

Community Impact

We see schools becoming energy-independent centers that can power evening classes, community workshops, and local enterprise.
